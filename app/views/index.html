GOV.UK One Login research   Public beta

To test if citizens are motivated to verify their identity after they complete the BSP online application form using the GOV.UK One Login service.


Prove identity page
Types of ID
Summary of what's being tested

1st round was to understand:

  • the impact of adding identity verification to BSP
  • how willing users are to prove their identity
  • how users feel about receiving a decision straight after proving identity
  • new item

Completed on 25.07.24

2nd round is to test:

  • with citizens with dependant children
  • with proxy citizens who have not applied for BSP
  • series of email notifications a citizen might recive

Completed on 19.09.24
