User Research 05.07.23   Private beta

This research is a second round of end to end testing with further iterations for service improvement.

New iterations to the form include:

  • updated GOV.UK guidance
  • an objective way to tell users how long the form will take
  • changed H1 for relationship question
  • a component to toggle to Welsh
  • a child benefit question included in the flow
  • DWP's find an address pattern
  • a new pattern to capture email for confirmation
  • mock decision emails
