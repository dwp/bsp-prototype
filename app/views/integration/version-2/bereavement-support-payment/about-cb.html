Do you get Child Benefit?

What is Child Benefit?

You get Child Benefit if you’re responsible for bringing up a child who is:

  • under 16
  • under 20 if they stay in approved education or training

It’s paid every 4 weeks.

You can get the higher rate of Bereavement Support Payment if you’re entitled to Child Benefit.

Go to www.gov.uk/child-benefit or call 0300 200 3100 to make a claim.
