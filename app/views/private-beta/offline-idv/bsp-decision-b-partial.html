You’ll get Bereavement Support Payment

You’ve proved your identity and your application for Bereavement Support Payment has been successful.

What happens next

We’ll make the first payment into your account as soon as possible.

We’ll send you a letter that explains:

  • what you’ll get
  • when we’ll pay you

This means your payment may arrive before your letter.

If you change your bank details, address or name

Contact us if you change your bank details, address or name.

When you call we will ask for your National Insurance number.
