We’ve sent you an email to confirm we’ve received your application.

Prove your identity

We can process your application faster if you prove your identity. This means we can start your payments sooner if your application is successful.

You can prove your identity with your GOV.UK One Login. If you do not already have one, you can create one.

If you cannot prove your identity

We’ll check your details based on the information we already have. We’ll contact you if we need more information.

You should get a decision by Monday 12 August.

If your application is successful, we’ll make a payment into your account as soon as possible.

Getting the higher rate of payment

If you’ve had Child Benefit entitlement since your partner’s death you could get the higher rate of Bereavement Support Payment.

Contact the Child Benefit Office if you think you could be entitled but have not yet applied. If you received Child Benefit in your partner’s name, you’ll need to make a new application.

Get help with your application

If you have any questions about your claim you can contact the Bereavement Support Payment team.
