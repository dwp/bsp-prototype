Which iPhone model do you have?

The iPhone 7 was released in 2016. If you purchased your smartphone after 2016, you likely have an iPhone 7 or newer.
How do I know which iPhone I have?
You can easily find the iPhone model you have by going to Settings > General > About and looking at the ‘Model Name’.
What if I have an iPhone SE?

If you have an iPhone SE from 2022 (3rd generation) or from 2020 (2nd generation), select ‘iPhone 7 or newer’.


If you have an iPhone SE from 2016 (1st generation), select ‘iPhone 6s or older’.
