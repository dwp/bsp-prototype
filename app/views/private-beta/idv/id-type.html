Tell us if you have one of the following types of photo ID

This will help us find the best way for you to prove your identity.

  • UK photocard driving licence

    You can use a full or provisional UK driving licence if it:

    • is a plastic photocard, not a paper licence
    • has not expired

  • UK passport

    You can use any UK passport if it has not expired.

  • Non-UK passport with a biometric chip

    You can use a non-UK passport if it:

    • has a biometric chip
    • has not expired

    To check if your passport has a biometric chip, look for the rectangular biometric chip symbol on the front cover.

  • UK biometric residence permit (BRP)

    You can use a UK biometric resident permit (BRP) that has not expired. You might have a BRP if you have a visa for longer than 6 months or indefinite leave to remain.

  • UK biometric residence card (BRC)

    This is also called a UK residence card. You can use a BRC that has not expired. You might have a BRC if youâ€™re in the UK as a family member of a citizen from a European Economic Area (EEA) country.

  • UK Frontier Worker permit (FWP)

    You can use an FWP that has not expired. You might have an FWP if you work in the UK but live in another country.
