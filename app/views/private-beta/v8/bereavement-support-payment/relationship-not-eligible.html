You cannot get Bereavement Support Payment

This is because you were not married to, in a civil partnership with, or living with your partner when they died.

To be able to get Bereavement Support Payment, when your partner died you must have been:

  • married or in a registered civil partnership
  • living together as though you were married
If you live in Northern Ireland you may be able to apply for other benefits.
Find other support

If you need emotional help you can contact Cruse Bereavement Support (opens in new tab).

If you want this decision in writing you can finish your application even though you cannot get Bereavement Support Payment.
