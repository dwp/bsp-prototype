You cannot get Bereavement Support Payment

This is because your partner died before Bereavement Support Payment became available.

You cannot apply for Bereavement Support Payment if your partner died before 6 April 2017.

You may be able to get Widowed Parent's Allowance instead.

If you live in Northern Ireland there's a different way to apply for Widowed Parent's Allowance.

If you need emotional help you can contact Cruse Bereavement Support (opens in new tab).

If you want this decision in writing you can finish your application even though you cannot get Bereavement Support Payment.
