How your payment is calculated

You’ll get the standard rate because you were married when your partner died.

You’ll get 2 monthly payments because you reach State Pension age on {{ "today" | duration(64, "days") | govukDate }}. Once you reach State Pension age, you cannot be paid Bereavement Support Payment.

Your letter also explains how your payments were calculated.

If you need to contact us

If you have any questions or your circumstances change, call the Bereavement Support Payment team on 0800 151 2012.

Financial help

To check if you can get financial help:

  • use a benefits calculator to check what you can claim at www.gov.uk/benefits-calculators
  • visit your local jobcentre for advice on benefits you could be entitled to
  • ask your local coucil if you are eligible for hlep to pay your Council Tax
